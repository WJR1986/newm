Welcome!

North East House Clearance is your local, trusted house clearance, waste removal and recycling service. Covering Alnwick and the surrounding areas of Northumberland.

We cover residential, commercial, green waste, and more. We pride ourselves on recycling and disposing responsibly, ethically, and fully licensed. We aim to recycle as much as possible and strive to do so.

We ensure a seamless experience from start to finish, leaving your space clear of waste and looking rejuvenated. Contact us today for a tailored solution to suit your needs.
